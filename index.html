Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform and services.

How accurate is the market insights data provided by RTI Consultant?

Our market insights are based on comprehensive data collection from multiple reliable sources, including government statistics, industry reports, and real-time market surveys. We regularly update our data and employ advanced analytics to ensure accuracy. While we strive for the highest level of precision, we recommend using our insights as a guide alongside your own business knowledge.

What methodology is used to generate regulatory compliance roadmaps?

Our regulatory compliance roadmaps are generated using a combination of expert legal knowledge and advanced AI. We maintain a comprehensive database of Rwandan business regulations that is regularly updated. When you submit your business details, our system analyzes applicable regulations and creates a customized roadmap based on your specific business type, industry, and objectives.

How does RTI Consultant ensure data privacy and security?

We take data security very seriously. All user data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security protocols and regularly conduct security audits. Your business information is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent, and we comply with all Rwandan data protection regulations.

What are the pricing and subscription options for using RTI Consultant?

RTI Consultant offers flexible pricing tiers to accommodate businesses of all sizes. We provide a basic free tier with limited features, and premium tiers starting at 50,000 RWF per month for more comprehensive insights and personalized guidance. Enterprise solutions with custom features are also available. All paid subscriptions can be managed through our secure IremboPay integration.

How does IREMBO integrate into the RTI Consultant platform?

RTI Consultant integrates seamlessly with IREMBO's payment system (IremboPay) to provide secure, convenient payment options. This integration allows you to pay for premium services using MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, VISA/Mastercard, or bank transfers. Additionally, we leverage IREMBO's identity verification capabilities to ensure secure account creation and management.

How do LLM models integrate into the RTI Consultant platform?

RTI Consultant utilizes advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) like Mistral and LLAMA to power our regulatory guidance and market analysis features. These AI models help analyze complex business regulations, generate customized compliance roadmaps, and extract meaningful insights from market data. The LLMs are continuously trained on updated Rwandan business regulations and market trends to provide accurate, relevant guidance.

What plans does RTI Consultant have for future development?

RTI Consultant is committed to continuous improvement. Our roadmap includes expanding our data sources, enhancing our AI capabilities, developing mobile applications, and creating more specialized industry-specific guidance. We also plan to introduce features for connecting businesses with relevant service providers and potential partners within the Rwandan ecosystem.
