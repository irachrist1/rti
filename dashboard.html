Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade my plan?

Upgrading is easy! Click on the "Upgrade Now" button on the Premium plan card, and you'll be guided through our secure checkout process powered by IREMBOPay. You can pay using MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, bank transfer, or credit/debit card.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your premium subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll still have access to premium features until the end of your current billing cycle, after which your account will revert to the Free plan.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes! When you choose annual billing, you get two months free, which is a 16.7% discount compared to monthly billing. The annual plan costs 500,000 RWF per year instead of 600,000 RWF when paying monthly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through IREMBOPay, which supports MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, VISA/Mastercard, and bank transfers. All payments are secure and encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my account information?

You can update your profile information by going to the "My Account" section from the sidebar navigation. Click on the "Profile" tab where you can edit your personal and company information.

How do I upgrade my subscription?

To upgrade your subscription, navigate to the "Subscription" section from the sidebar. You'll see different plan options available. Click on the "Upgrade Now" button under the Premium Plan or "Contact Sales" for the Enterprise Plan. Follow the instructions to complete your payment.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Go to "My Account" and select the "Subscription" tab. Click on "Cancel Plan" and follow the prompts. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle, after which your account will revert to the Free plan.

How do I generate custom market insights?

To generate custom market insights, go to the "Market Insights" section and scroll down to the "Get Customized Market Insights" form. Select your industry, location, and time period, then click "Generate Insights." The platform will analyze the data and provide you with tailored market information.

How does the Regulatory AI Assistant work?

The Regulatory AI Assistant is an intelligent tool that answers your questions about business regulations in Rwanda. Navigate to the "Regulatory Guidance" section and scroll down to the assistant. Type your question in the input field or select one of the suggested queries. The AI will analyze your question and provide relevant regulatory information based on the latest data.

I'm having trouble loading certain sections of the dashboard. What should I do?

If you're experiencing loading issues, try these steps:

  1. Refresh your browser
  2. Clear your browser cache
  3. Try a different browser
  4. Check your internet connection
  5. If the problem persists, contact our support team through the "Contact Us" tab in the Help & Support section

How secure is my data on the platform?

We take data security very seriously. Your data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols both in transit and at rest. We implement multiple security layers including secure authentication, regular security audits, and compliance with international data protection regulations. Your business information is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I export data from the dashboard?

Yes, you can export data from various sections of the dashboard. Look for export buttons (usually with icons like or ) in the header of each section or card. The free plan allows 3 exports per month, while premium and enterprise plans offer unlimited exports.
