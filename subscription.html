Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade my plan?

Upgrading is easy! Click on the "Upgrade Now" button on the Premium plan card, and you'll be guided through our secure checkout process powered by IREMBOPay. You can pay using MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, bank transfer, or credit/debit card.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your premium subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll still have access to premium features until the end of your current billing cycle, after which your account will revert to the Free plan.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes! When you choose annual billing, you get two months free, which is a 16.7% discount compared to monthly billing. The annual plan costs 500,000 RWF per year instead of 600,000 RWF when paying monthly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through IREMBOPay, which supports MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, VISA/Mastercard, and bank transfers. All payments are secure and encrypted using industry-standard protocols.
